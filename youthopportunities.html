    1.Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program

    This program is aimed towards girls entering 11th or 12th grade.
    Dates: Varied Location: Varied
    This is a introductory computer science course that teaches girls the fundamentals of computer programming. They spend weeks learning about robotics,web design, python, javascript, and the final 2 weeks are spent entirety working on an app of their own where they use all the skills they've earned.
    https://girlswhocode.com/summer-immersion-programs/

    3.Read, Write, Discover Tutor

    This program is aimed high school students
    Dates: Varied Location:Varied
    Tutors will be expected to meet up with one or more children 2 times a week for 2 hours each. The program is very flexible as they understand that people go on vacations. The locations are at public libraries and there are also positions as interns who manage all the tutors and give them day plans.
    http://www.readwritediscover.org/volunteer/
